## Active Objectives

**OBJ-1 - Secure paid work in Halden.**
Find guard, escort, retainer, enforcement, or similar armed work before Kael's coin runs thin. Possible patrons include institutions, guild interests, households, merchants, or civic authorities, but none is guaranteed at start.
Status: In progress. Merchant escort contact failed after the Tollen Var altercation. Kael has secured day-hire night security work at the Order hospice/lower passage.

**OBJ-2 - Seek a position more stable than wandering.**
Convert Kael's martial skill into durable standing, patronage, or regular employment without losing autonomy or being discarded as a disposable outsider.
Status: Active. The Order job may be a foothold, but it is dangerous, low-trust, and not yet stable patronage.

**OBJ-3 - Manage temper and authority risk.**
Avoid turning first contact with Halden's authorities into a public confrontation, especially given the recent execution of an outsider who attacked the Order.
Status: Strained. Kael lost his temper with Tollen Var and scattered his books in public, but has so far obeyed Sister Avel and Brant while on Order duty.

**OBJ-4 - Understand where violence is useful, dangerous, or being baited.**
Read Halden's institutional pressures well enough to decide when the sword solves a problem and when it makes Kael the problem.
Status: Active. First watch has shown that posture, voice, and line discipline matter more than drawn steel inside the hospice.

**OBJ-5 - Survive and control the lower-passage watch.**
Hold the family/patient line, avoid worsening possible fever exposure, and respond to the wrong-stair intruder alert without abandoning the post or drawing steel without cause.
Status: Active.
